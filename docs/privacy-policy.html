Privacy Policy

LocalHabit All · Effective date: April 29, 2026

LocalHabit All is designed with a privacy-first, local-only approach. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may access, how data is stored, and how your privacy is protected.

1. Data Storage

LocalHabit All stores your data locally on your device. This includes habits, streaks, focus records, journal entries, to-do items, reminders, settings, and locally imported media.

  • No account is required.
  • No cloud sync is required.
  • No remote server is used to store your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information through a backend service. The app may process the following information only on your device in order to provide core functionality:

  • Habit tracking data and streak history
  • Focus timer sessions and statistics
  • Journal text and locally selected images
  • To-do items and completion status
  • App preferences such as theme, reminders, and lock settings

3. Permissions

Depending on which features you use, LocalHabit All may request access to:

  • Photos: to let you choose images for journal entries
  • Notifications: to deliver habit reminders and focus timer alerts

These permissions are optional and can be managed at any time in iOS Settings.

4. Backups and Exports

If you choose to export or back up your data, the exported file is created by you on your device and saved to a location you choose, such as the Files app. Imported backups are processed locally on your device.

5. Security

LocalHabit All uses device-local protections to help secure stored data, including encrypted local storage for supported app data and optional app lock or journal lock features when enabled.

No method of storage is absolutely secure, but we are committed to minimizing data exposure by keeping your information on-device whenever possible.

6. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

  • No third-party advertising SDKs are used.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs are used.
  • No cross-app or cross-site tracking is performed.

7. Widgets

If you use Home Screen or Lock Screen widgets, limited app data needed to render widget content may be shared locally between the app and its widget extension on your device.

8. Children’s Privacy

LocalHabit All does not knowingly collect personal data from children through online services, because the app does not rely on a remote account or cloud data collection system.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app improvements, legal requirements, or feature changes. The updated version will be posted at this same URL.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the support information provided on the App Store listing or project repository.
